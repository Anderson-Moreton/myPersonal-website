About me

My name is Anderson Moreton and I am from São Paulo. I graduated in Systems Analysis from FMU - Centro Universitário das Faculdades Metropolitanas Unidas de São Paulo. Since I was a child, I have always loved technology, video games, and Rock and Roll. I had my first contact with programming later, but it was enough for me to fully enter this world. I started university in 2021 and since then I have been studying and learning more about the world of technology.

Because I had to balance studies and work, I always had to prioritize my job, as I needed to support my home and family. I always did this without stopping my studies, because changing my career would require a lot of study and dedication. We know it is not easy, and change always brings some fear and discomfort.

But I was patient and worked hard, because I knew an opportunity would come. The first opportunity came in 2024, to work with databases in a casino in Portugal, where I live and work now.
